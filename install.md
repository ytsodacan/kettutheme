# iOS On-Device Tutorial

1. First you will need to go to [https://sign.applep12.com](https://sign.applep12.com), you should see this:
![Step 1](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2903.png?raw=true)

2. Press the **Get Device's UDID** button, once the configuration popup shows up press **Allow**.
![Step 2](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2904.png?raw=true)

3. Now you will need to go to **Settings > General > VPN and Device Management** and press on the new profile.

4. Press **Install** and enter your password, you will need to press **Install** again, it will redirect you back to the page and you should see a number in the UDID field. 
![Step 4](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2905.png?raw=true)

5. Press on the **Cert** dropdown and change it to the bottom one, it may be different than the photo.
![Step 5](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2906.png?raw=true)

6. Make sure the **IPA** dropdown is set to **Feather_V(version)**.
![Step 6](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2907.png?raw=true)

7. Now press **"Start Sign IPA"**

8. And then press the **Install** button. In the popup press **Install** again.
![Step 8](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2908.png?raw=true)

9. You should see a new app named **"Feather"** in your app library.

10. Open the app, you might need to allow it in **VPN and Device Management**.

11. Once you are in the app, press the **Add Source** button. Paste in this link and press save:
```
https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/raw/refs/heads/main/source.json
```
![Step 11a](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2909.png?raw=true)
![Step 11b](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2910.png?raw=true)

12. Press **Radium** and then press **Get** next to the app. 
![Step 12a](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2911.png?raw=true)
![Step 12b](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2912.png?raw=true)

13. Go into the **Library** tab and press **Sign**.
![Step 13](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2913.png?raw=true)

14. Press **Start Signing**.
![Step 14](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2914.png?raw=true)

15. Press the **Day Counter** and it will start installing.
![Step 15a](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2915.png?raw=true)
![Step 15b](https://github.com/ytsodacan/kettutheme/blob/main/IMG_2916.png?raw=true)

16. There you go! You have now installed Radium for iOS.
